## Notes

- Chapter headers inside the files now carry the final sequential numbering
  (The Keeper = 15, The Terrace = 16, ... After = 24). Filenames retain their
  working names.
- Part-division headings (`# PART ONE: SIGNAL`, etc.) appear at the top of the
  first chapter of each part: ch01, ch07, ch13, ch19.
- Interludes are unnumbered in the book and set in their documentary formats
  (journal, maintenance log, intelligence report, notebook).

## Typographic conventions

- **Scene break:** `---` (horizontal rule).
- **Voice-gap / deliberate silence:** a line containing a single `*`. Used in
  Part Three's dual-voice passages where neither the chip's voice nor the body's
  can speak. Typeset as extra vertical white space, not as a printed asterisk,
  in the final book.
- **Chip annotations:** italic, usually bracketed when they interrupt mid-clause:
  `*[correction: ...]*`.
- **Em-dash policy:** Parts One–Three use the em dash freely (the broken,
  interrupting registers). Chapter 24 and the epilogue deliberately favor commas
  and colons — the integrated voice connects where the fractured voice broke.
  This asymmetry is intentional; do not "normalize" it in either direction.
